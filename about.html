Meet the Socialstar team

At Socialstar, we believe that building true relationships with your coworkers is the key to being engaged, happy, and successful at work.

Socialstar was founded on a shared desire to help people connect, as well as the realisation that solid relationships at work are the foundation of a successful team. We are creating a corporation that embraces those values while instilling them in others.

We are a people-oriented company. Socialstar was formed in 2019 on the concept that by developing their own unique software, people may solve their own unique problems. Come assist us in shaping the future of work and software!

Matthew Spacie
Founder

Also Founder and CEO of Magic Bus since 1999. Previously, he was a co founder of cleartrip and COO of Cox and Kings. Matthew is an Ashoka Fellow, a TED Fellow and more recently an Asia Centre for Social Entrepreneurship and Philanthropy (ACSEP) Fellow In 2007, he was awarded an MBE for services to children in the Commonwealth.

Open Roles

As an early part of the Socialstar team, you will have the opportunity (and obligation!) to help shape our culture while producing products that will impact millions of businesses worldwide.
  • Senior Frontend Developer

    As a Senior Frontend Developer, you'll be working on a distributed team with diverse skills across the tech stack. We build features, enhancements, experiments, and deliver sustainable, quality software in an agile environment.

    What you'll help us achieve:

    >  Write, test, instrument, document, and maintain quality software.

    >  Review code and provide thoughtful, constructive feedback for your team.

    >  Collaborate closely with Product, Design, and other developers to build new features.

    >  Analyze how your features are performing in production to learn and make the product better.

    >  Support a highly available service.


    Who you are:

    >  Have deep expertise in ReactJS & its internals.

    >  Have experience in profiling, Monitoring, and improving front-end performance.

    >  Have high empathy in everything you do.

    >  Have built web experiences and systems at scale.

    >  Enjoy and thrive in a remote-first, distributed work culture.

    >  Are highly self-directed and take initiative to go above and beyond expectations.


  • Junior Frontend Developer

    What does a Junior Developer do at Socialstar?

    >  Experienced in building end-to-end ecommerce web applications and features using JavaScript and React.

    >  Willing to work in a team to ensure successful delivery.

    >  Learning and growth are key to our culture, so we will be looking for examples of where you have challenged yourself and learned a new tool, skill, or way of working.


    What we'd love to see in your work history:

    >  Experience in the following technologies: React, Modern Javascript, TypeScript, Sass, HTML5 and CSS3

    >  Proven experience in deploying React applications using pipelines from integration all the way to production

    >  An enthusiasm for hands-on labour, as well as a willingness to roll up your sleeves to get the job done

    >  The capacity to survive in a fast-paced work environment with constantly changing difficulties

    >  The power to guide your own work.

    >  A strong interest in your trade, with a constant desire to learn more and develop oneself

    >  The capacity to wear several hats, identify methods to add value, and a willingness to do what is required

    >  Contribute to your team via pair programming, code reviews, knowledge sharing and team demos

    >  A demonstrated capacity to be effective while working remotely, as well as an interest in remote work situations.

    >  Fully test your applications using unit, integration, and functional testing


  • UI/UX Designer

    What does a UI/UX Designer do at Socialstar?

    >  Lead the design of Socialstar's web apps.

    >  Collaborate to provide a comprehensive user experience and to bridge overlapping functionality amongst Socialstar's features and platforms.

    >  Based on input from colleagues and data insights, iterate on design solutions and concepts.

    >  Work closely with the Engineering Team to put your ideas into action.


    What we'd love to see in your work history:

    >  A superb design aesthetic

    >  A love of software and the ability to turn ideas into engaging product experiences

    >  An enthusiasm for hands-on labour, as well as a willingness to roll up your sleeves to get the job done

    >  The capacity to survive in a fast-paced work environment with constantly changing difficulties

    >  The power to guide your own work.

    >  A strong interest in your trade, with a constant desire to learn more and develop oneself

    >  The capacity to wear several hats, identify methods to add value, and a willingness to do what is required

    >  A willingness to assist our team in its constant improvement. We want you to teach us something new!

    >  A demonstrated capacity to be effective while working remotely, as well as an interest in remote work situations.

    >  An in-depth knowledge of Figma, including auto-layout and variations. You get extra points if you've ever worked on tools that makes use of Figma's API!
